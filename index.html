Who We Are

A Family Business Built on Glass

American Glass Experts Inc. is a family-owned glass company based in Reseda, CA — serving the San Fernando Valley and all of Southern California. Our team brings more than 20 years of combined hands-on glazing experience to every job.

We specialize in custom glass design, storefronts, and glass partitions — and we work closely with contractors, architects, and homeowners to make sure every project is finished on time and within budget. Whether it's a single cracked window or a full commercial installation, we treat every job the same: show up, do it right, leave it clean.

You just lay back and wait to see the finished product. Our team figures it out.
